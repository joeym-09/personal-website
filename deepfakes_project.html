Problem: Fake Media

The widespread concerns regarding privacy and misinformation have shunned the spot-light on deepfakes, which are fake media produced using algorithms such as autoencoders and Generative Adversarial Networks. Deepfakes can be an especially dangerous tool and have been previously used for fraud, scams, voter manipualtion, and more.

Solution: Using Computer Vision to Detect Deepfakes

I developed a computer vision algorithm to detect deepfakes. Specifically, I used a pre-trained convolutional neural network known as MobileNetV2 and trained it on top of another dataset in order for it to detect fake media from real media.
